Welcome to Precis!

Precis (properly Pr√©cis, pronounced "pray-see") is a extensibility-oriented RSS reader that can use LLMs to summarize and synthesize information from numerous different sources, with an emphasis on timely delivery of information via notifications.

In order to use Precis, you'll have to configure it correctly.

Most functions in Precis are managed by handlers - namely, LLM functions, content retrieval, and notification. Press the button below to go to the Settings page to configure handlers. This step is optional - content retrieval is handled by Playwright out of the box, and we provide default no-op handlers for LLMs and notification. However - of course, you will get more out of Precis if you configure handlers for these features.

Go to Settings

Once you're finished configuring handlers, you'll need to configure at least one feed to start using Precis. Click the button below to configure a feed.
